ABOUT

Hi! My name is Sacha van Alfen.
I am a Graphic Designer who recently graduated from the Royal Academy of Arts with a great interest in (motion) typography, print and coding. I like to work digital as much as I like to work with print. Projects I work on vary from a chatbot about the two faces of nostalgia, a website about a stamp collection published in Iran during the Iran - Iraq war, a publication about the importance of the bee, a game about the ecological footprint of animal agricult and more.
