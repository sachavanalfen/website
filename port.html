Sacha van Alfen
Haagse Port
Every year the city vineyard of the Hague (Haagse stadswijngaard) ask an artist to design the label of a specific wine they produced that year. This time it was a port wine. Together with Ivi Apostolatos and Merel Meijer we designed a label inspired by the beautiful Portugese tiles. Both Portugal and the Hague have a bird as their symbol: the rooster and the stork. From a set of abstract shapes together with the grid from the tiles we made a graphic illustration of the birds who together form the center of the label.

ABOUT

Hi! My name is Sacha van Alfen.
I am a Graphic Designer who recently graduated from the Royal Academy of Arts with a great interest in (motion) typography, print and coding. I like to work digital as much as I like to work with print. Projects I work on vary from a chatbot about the two faces of nostalgia, a website about a stamp collection published in Iran during the Iran - Iraq war, a publication about the importance of the bee, a game about the ecological footprint of animal agricult and more.
